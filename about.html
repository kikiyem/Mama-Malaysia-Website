About


Mama Malaysia is comfy restaurant with the purpose to serve classic Malaysian dishes to the heart of NYC.

Mama Malaysia is named after our founder and her relationship with her daughter. She started cooking traditional dishes to her daughter with the goal of introducing her to the culture and food she grew up with in Malaysia. That love influenced her to open Mama Malaysia and share the joy with everyone.
